United for Efficiency (U4E) provides a Model regulation guidelines for energy performance requirements for distribution transformers (10/2019) is provided at https://united4efficiency.org/resources/model-regulation-guidelines-for-energy-performance-requirements-for-distribution-transformers/
It provides a voluntary guidance to assist Governments in developing and emerging economies that are considering a regulatory or legislative framework for minimum energy performance standards.
The model considers distribution power transformers in the 5 - 3150 kVA range to the international best practice energy performance level. This model regulation does not cover transformers outside the mentioned range or transformers designed for special applications.

The policy level to be/ already implemented in the country regarding the mannufacturing of efficient transformers:
Level 1 basic energy performance option: offering a draft policy framework to other countries that do have a significant domestic distribution power transformer
Level 2 high energy performance option: offering a draft policy framework designed to leapfrog directly to high energy-performance, which is the international best practice policy. This is suitable for countries that do not have a significant domestic distribution power transformer manufacturing industry and import almost all of them.
